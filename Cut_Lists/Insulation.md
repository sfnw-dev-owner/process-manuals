EXTERIOR INSULATION

Cut and load in this order, back to front of the insulation cart, top
level.

> \(8\) 24 in x 45 ½ in Mark “Middle Roof” (2 pieces from one bat)
>
> \(4\) 18 in x 45 ½ in Mark “End Roof” (2 pieces from one bat)

Save some of the 12 in cutoff pieces for use as insulation for
structural triangles; small pieces should be put into trash. There is a
black plastic bag for the saved insulation, next to the insulation
storage area.

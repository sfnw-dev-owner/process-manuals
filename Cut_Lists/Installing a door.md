# Installing a door – checklist (rtfm)

## Prepare:
- Reinforce door trim corners – brown screws
- Be sure to remove plastic feet from bottom of door frame
- Flexible flashing across bottom of opening and 8" up each side (see instruction manual)

## Install:
- Tilt door into opening, bottom first. (Remove plug holding the door shut first.)
- Shims to align door frame with steel door
- Parallel across top of door
- Parallel on latch side of door
- \#10 Screws to anchor frame to building. Shim behind door frame at each location:
  - above top hinge
  - high on latch side of frame
  - above bottom hinge
  - low on latch side of frame
  - above middle hinge
  - two screws, above and below latch cutout. See instruction book for positioning. Aim for 1/8" gap between door and frame on this side.
  - two screws up through top of door frame. Use plywood to fill most of the opening.
- Three screws through flooring into platform. Use 3" plated screws to match ADA threshold

## Finish:
- Remove one screw from each hinge, and replace with 3" plated screw.
- Trim visible flexible flashing from either side of exterior door trim and from under threshold.
- Trim all shims to be even with interior plywood walls
- Remove weatherstripping from sides and top of door frame, and save these.
- If wiper comes off the bottom of the door, remove it completely, and save with other weatherstripping.

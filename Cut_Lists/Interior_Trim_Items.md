### Notes

- Verify width between finished window liner and midpoint of door frame, and rip 9-1/2 plank to this width.  Remaining strip can be used behind the door.
- Match plank widths of Wide Trim for Vent Cap, so that the seam will be less obvious
- Cut 13" blocks under front chop saw in half for Vent Blocks
